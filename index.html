About Hasib

Entrepreneur

As the co-founder of Craftsmen, a software farm that provides scalable cloud solutions, I have had the opportunity to lead the company's growth from a team of 5 to over 70 people. We have earned a reputation as one of Bangladesh’s best software farms to work and grow in, and have been recognized with awards for best startup company and best outsourcing company from BASIS.


During this period, I have had the privilege of working with some of the most gifted engineers, recruiting and mentoring many talented individuals, and having screened over 5k candidates in the process. From that experience, I learned about the traits recruiters look for and how candidates fall short to demonstrate their fit for the role.

Software Artisan

In my 7+ years of experience as a software engineer, I have architected software for one of the largest petroleum companies in Scandinavia, developed features for the world’s largest online freight marketplace, and contributed to systems used by some of the biggest names in the media industry.


In this short journey, I have learned what elevates someone from being a good to a great software engineer, as well as how to avoid pitfalls that could impede one’s progress.

    The workshop will be hosted online via the Google Meet. We will also use Discord for communication.

    Unfortunately, it will not be recorded. However, you will be able to access any shared resources through Discord.

    Yes, it is encouraged for you to ask questions. There will be a designated time for Q&A where you can ask your own questions or vote for others’ questions to be answered. The questions with the most votes will be answered within the allocated time.
